Kanchipuram

AS BABU SAH

Kanchipuram is renowned as "Babu Sah of South India" for its rich heritage in silk weaving, especially the world-famous Kanchipuram silk sarees, symbolizing elegance and tradition.It is very famous for its rich cultural heritage and ancient architecture. This place is world-famous for Kanchipuram silk sarees, handwoven with pure mulberry silk. It was once the capital of the Pallava dynasty, full of historical importance. The city has temples like Kamakshi Amman Temple, Ekambareswarar Temple, and Varadaraja Perumal Temple, which attract lakhs of devotees.
